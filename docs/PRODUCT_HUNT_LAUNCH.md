### Full Product Description

#### Problem Statement

Indie authors are juggling multiple sales platforms. Amazon KDP, Gumroad, Apple Books, Google Play—each with its own dashboard, its own metrics, its own login.

Every morning, you're logging into 5 different sites just to see how many books you sold yesterday. You're copying numbers into spreadsheets. You're spending 30 minutes on admin work instead of writing.

And you still don't have a clear picture of your business. You don't know which books are performing best. You don't know if your pricing is competitive. You don't know what to focus on next.

#### Solution

AuthorStack brings all your book sales into one beautiful dashboard. See all your sales, all your revenue, all your data—in one place.

No more logging into multiple platforms. No more manual data entry. No more spreadsheets. Just one dashboard that shows you everything you need to run your book business.

#### Key Features

✅ **Real-Time Sales Dashboard** - See all your sales across all platforms instantly

✅ **Revenue Tracking** - Track revenue by platform, by book, and over time

✅ **Competitor Monitoring** - Track competitor prices and get alerts when they change

✅ **Launch Planning** - Create launch checklists with tasks and reminders

✅ **Multi-Platform Support** - Amazon KDP, Gumroad, Apple Books, Google Play, and more

✅ **Data Export** - Export all your data as CSV anytime

✅ **Mobile Ready** - Check your sales on the go

#### Pricing

**During Beta:** Completely free

**After Beta (Q2 2026):**
- **Starter:** $9/month (up to 5 books)
- **Pro:** $29/month (unlimited books, advanced analytics)
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing

All beta users get 50% off forever.

#### Roadmap

We're actively building:
- AI-powered insights and recommendations
- Advanced analytics (cohort analysis, funnel tracking)
- Marketing tools (email templates, social scheduler)
- Community features (connect with other authors)
- API access for integrations
- Native mobile apps

#### Call to Action

## 💬 Expected Questions & Answers

### Q: How do you access my sales data?
**A:** We use official APIs from each platform. You authorize us, and we sync your data daily. You can revoke access anytime. We never ask for passwords.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes. We use enterprise-grade security with encryption at rest and in transit. Row-level security ensures you only see your own data. More details in our [security docs].

### Q: What platforms do you support?
**A:** Currently: Amazon KDP, Gumroad, Apple Books, Google Play. We're adding more based on demand. You can also manually import CSV files.

### Q: Will this always be free?
**A:** During beta, yes. After launch, we'll have a freemium model. All beta users get 50% off forever.

### Q: Can I export my data?
**A:** Yes. You can export all your data as CSV anytime. Your data is yours.

### Q: How often is data updated?
**A:** We sync daily. Real-time sync is on the roadmap.

### Q: What about a mobile app?
**A:** We're web-first for now. The dashboard is fully responsive. Native apps are on the roadmap.

### Q: Can I use this for other products?
**A:** Not yet. We're focused on indie authors. Let us know if you have other use cases!

### Q: How is this different from [competitor]?
**A:** We're built specifically for indie authors. We focus on simplicity and the features that matter most. We're also indie-founder-friendly with transparent pricing.

### Q: What's your pricing after beta?
**A:** Starter ($9/month, up to 5 books), Pro ($29/month, unlimited), Enterprise (custom). All beta users get 50% off forever.

### Q: How do I get started?
**A:** Sign up at authorstack.com, connect your first platform, and you'll see your sales instantly.
