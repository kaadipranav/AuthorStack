## 💬 Expected Questions & Answers

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes. We use Supabase for database security with row-level security policies. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share your data with third parties. More details in our [privacy policy].

### Q: How do you access my platform data?
**A:** You authorize us through each platform's official API. We never ask for passwords. You can revoke access anytime.

### Q: What if my platform isn't supported yet?
**A:** We're starting with the most popular platforms (Amazon KDP, Gumroad, Apple Books, Google Play). We're adding more based on demand. You can also manually import CSV files.

### Q: Will this always be free?
**A:** During beta, yes. After launch, we'll have a freemium model. All beta users get 50% off forever.

### Q: Can I export my data?
**A:** Yes. You can export all your data as CSV anytime. Your data is yours.

### Q: What about mobile app?
**A:** We're web-first for now. The dashboard is fully responsive and works great on mobile. Native apps are on the roadmap.

### Q: How often is data updated?
**A:** We sync data daily. Real-time sync is on the roadmap.

### Q: Is there a free tier after beta?
**A:** Yes. Free tier will include 1 book, basic analytics, and competitor tracking. Paid tiers unlock unlimited books and advanced features.

### Q: Can I use this for other products (not books)?
**A:** Not yet. We're focused on indie authors first. But the architecture is designed to expand. Let us know if you have other use cases!

### Q: How is this different from [competitor]?
**A:** We're built specifically for indie authors. We focus on simplicity and the features that matter most. We're also indie-founder-friendly with transparent pricing and no vendor lock-in.
